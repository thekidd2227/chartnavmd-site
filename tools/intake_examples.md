# ARCG Systems — Intake Persona Examples

Here are three distinct intake personas to test the v4.1 GovCon prompt. You can copy-paste these directly into Manus or Claude to generate the JSON reports.

---

## Example 1: GovCon Small Concern (Compliance & Access Focus)

**Business Name:** Apex Logistics & Supply LLC
**Industry:** Government Contracting (Defense Logistics)
**Contact Name:** Sarah Jenkins
**Work Email:** s.jenkins@apexlogistics.gov
**Biggest Problem:** "We are an SDVOSB but we keep losing bids to larger primes because our past performance data is scattered across spreadsheets and we can't generate compliant capability statements fast enough when a solicitation drops."
**Problem Area:** Proposal Execution & Federal Access
**Current Tools:** Excel, Word, basic email
**CRM:** None
**First Automation Target:** "I need a system that takes our past contract data and automatically formats it into a compliant capability statement so we can respond to RFQs faster."
**Volume:** 3-5 RFQs per month
**Desired Result:** "Stop missing bid deadlines and actually leverage our SDVOSB status to win set-asides."

## Example 2: GovCon Large Concern (Subcontractor & Vendor Management Focus)

**Business Name:** Vanguard Facilities Operations
**Industry:** Government Contracting (Base Operations Support)
**Contact Name:** David Rossi
**Work Email:** drossi@vanguardops.com
**Biggest Problem:** "We manage 4 military bases and use over 30 different subcontractors (janitorial, pest control, HVAC). Right now, my PMs spend 20 hours a week just chasing subs for their compliance reports and insurance certs to satisfy our prime contract requirements."
**Problem Area:** Vendor Coordination & Compliance Reporting
**Current Tools:** Salesforce, Outlook, SharePoint
**CRM:** Salesforce
**First Automation Target:** "Automate the collection and tracking of subcontractor compliance documents so we don't get hit with a cure notice from the DoD."
**Volume:** 30+ active subcontractors, 100+ compliance docs monthly
**Desired Result:** "A centralized dashboard where I can see exactly which sub is out of compliance before the government auditor does."

## Example 3: Commercial AI Bot (Lead Capture & Routing Focus)

**Business Name:** Horizon Property Management
**Industry:** Commercial Real Estate
**Contact Name:** Michael Chen
**Work Email:** mchen@horizonproperties.com
**Biggest Problem:** "We get 50+ inquiries a day for our commercial spaces, but my leasing team is always out on tours. By the time they reply to an email, the prospect has already booked a tour with a competitor. We are bleeding leads."
**Problem Area:** Lead Response & Booking Automation
**Current Tools:** HubSpot, Calendly, Gmail
**CRM:** HubSpot
**First Automation Target:** "An AI agent that can instantly reply to inbound leasing inquiries, answer basic questions about square footage and pricing, and book a tour directly on the agent's Calendly."
**Volume:** 50-75 inbound leads per day
**Desired Result:** "Zero missed leads. Every inquiry gets a response in under 2 minutes and qualified prospects are booked on the calendar automatically."
