01Operational Diagnosis
Root Cause Analysis

Axiom Automation Co.'s core failure is a missing standardization layer between the sales close and project delivery — the agency is transitioning from GovCon to commercial work and manually setting up every project from scratch, which means every new client engagement begins with a blank slate, a CRM record, and a founder doing 4–8 hours of manual configuration. The problem is not effort or intention — it is the absence of a triggered, templated system that converts a closed deal into a live project automatically. The current model is not an agency workflow — it is a freelancer workflow at agency scale. Until the gap between CRM close and project kickoff is automated, headcount cannot be reduced from the critical path, capacity is permanently capped by the founder's available hours, and no meaningful scale is achievable.
